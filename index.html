About Clutchly

Clutchly is your personal sports diary for Formula 1, NFL, and NBA. Track games, rate performances, write reviews, and connect with fellow sports fans. Build your ultimate collection of sports memories with AI-powered recaps and custom lists.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a game to my diary?

Tap the plus button in your diary, select your sport (F1/NFL/NBA), choose the season, find your game, and add your rating and review.

Can I track games from previous seasons?

Yes! You can add games from any available season for Formula 1 (1950+), NFL (2000+), and NBA (1990+).

How do AI recaps work?

After adding a game, tap on it to view an AI-generated summary with key highlights and drama from that specific game or race.

How do I follow friends?

Go to the Friends tab, tap the person icon, search for friends, and send follow requests. Once accepted, you'll see their activity in your feed.

Can I create custom lists?

Absolutely! Create lists for favorite teams, memorable games, or any theme. Mix games from F1, NFL, and NBA in the same list.

How do I share lists with friends?

Open any list, tap the share button, select friends to share with, and choose their permission level (view, edit, or admin).

My app isn't syncing properly

Try pulling down to refresh on any screen. If issues persist, restart the app or contact support.

I can't find a specific game

Make sure you've selected the correct sport and season. Some older games may not be available in our database.

How do I delete a diary entry?

Long press on any diary entry and select delete, or swipe left on the entry.

Troubleshooting

App crashes or freezes

Force close the app and reopen it. If problems persist, restart your device or reinstall the app.

Login issues

Check your internet connection and try again. For Google Sign-In issues, make sure you're using the correct Google account.

Missing data

Your data is stored in the cloud. Pull down to refresh or sign out and back in to restore your diary and lists.

Privacy & Data

Clutchly respects your privacy. Your diary entries and personal data are securely stored and only shared with friends you explicitly follow or accept requests from. We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties.


For our complete privacy policy, please visit our app's privacy settings or contact us directly.
