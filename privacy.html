Privacy Policy

Simple and transparent

We keep your information private and secure. We don't sell your data to anyone.

What information do we collect?

When you use Clutchly, we collect:

  • Your name and email (from your account)
  • Your profile picture (if you add one)
  • The games you add to your diary
  • Your reviews and ratings
  • Lists you create
  • Who you follow and who follows you

We cannot see your passwords. Authentication is handled securely by Google or Apple.

How do we use your information?

We use your information to:

  • Show your diary entries and lists
  • Let you follow friends and see their activity
  • Generate AI summaries of games
  • Send you notifications (if you enable them)
  • Fix bugs and improve the app

Do we share your information?

We never share your personal information with third parties for advertising or marketing.

With other users:

  • Your name and profile picture are visible to people who follow you
  • Your diary entries are shared with your followers
  • Lists you share are visible to people you give access to

How do we protect your information?

  • All data is encrypted when sent over the internet
  • Your information is stored on secure cloud servers
  • We regularly update security measures
  • Only you and people you follow can see your content

Your control over your data

You can:

  • Edit or delete any diary entry or list
  • Change your profile information anytime
  • Control who can follow you
  • Delete your account completely
  • Export your data

If you delete your account, all your data is permanently removed.

Changes to this policy

If we change this privacy policy, we'll update this page and let you know through the app. The date below shows when it was last updated.
